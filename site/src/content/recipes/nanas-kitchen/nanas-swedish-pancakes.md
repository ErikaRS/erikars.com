title: "NaNa\u2019s Swedish Pancakes"
---

- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup flour
- 1 egg
- Butter

Whisk together all ingredients.

Heat butter in a hot frying pan. Pour in the batter by spoonfuls. As soon as you see bubbles appearing on tops of pancakes, flip them over and cook for another 2 minutes until brown.

Enjoy with maple syrup.
