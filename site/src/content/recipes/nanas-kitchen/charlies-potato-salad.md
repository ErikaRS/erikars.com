title: "Charlie\u2019s Potato Salad"
---

- 5 medium potatoes, cooked and peeled
- 1 teaspoon salt Dash of white pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped green onion 2 tablespoons chopped pimento
- 5 hard boiled eggs, diced
- 11/4 cup mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup diced celery

Put all the ingredients together in a bowl and chill at least 2 hours.
