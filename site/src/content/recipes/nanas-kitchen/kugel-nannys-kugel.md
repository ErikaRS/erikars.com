- 1 cup cottage cheese
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1/2 pound cream cheese
- 5 eggs, beaten
- 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 pound wide egg noodles, cooked
- 2 cups chopped apples
- 1 cup raisins (optional)
- Cinnamon/sugar mix (optional)

Heat the oven to 350 degrees.

Mix cottage cheese, sour cream, cream cheese, eggs, butter, honey, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl until well blended. Add noodles, apples, raisins, and mix well. Spoon into greased 9Ã—13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar if desired.

Bake for 50-55 minutes or until set.

Serves 12.
