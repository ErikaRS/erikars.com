title: Creamed Tuna
---

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 can tuna
- 1 package frozen peas, defrosted

Make a roux with the butter and flour. Slowly add the milk and whisk until combined and smooth. Bring to a boil. Add the tuna and peas.

Serve on toast or with mashed potatoes.
