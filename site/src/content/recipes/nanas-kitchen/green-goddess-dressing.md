title: Green Goddess Dressing
---

- 1/2 bunch parsley, cut fine
- Green part of green onion, cut fine
- 1 can long anchovies
- 2 tablespoons tarragon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise

Mix together.
