title: Thousand Island Dressing
---

- 11/2 cup salad dressing
- 4-5 tablespoons chili sauce
- 3 tablespoons ketchup
- 1/2 green pepper, finely chopped
- 1 small can pimento, finely chopped
- 1 bunch green onions cut up
- 1 tablespoon paprika

Combine all ingredients.

If tarter taste is desired add juice of 1/2 lemon.
