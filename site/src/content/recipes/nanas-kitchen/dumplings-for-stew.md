title: Dumplings for Stew
---

- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 1/3 cup milk

Mix together the flour, baking powder, salt.

Beat the eggs with the milk. Add the flour mixture. Add a little more milk if needed to make a drop batter.
