- 1/4 pound (1 stick) unsalted butter
- 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
- 1 6 ounce package chocolate chips 1 6 ounce package butterscotch chips
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 1 cup shredded coconut
- 1 14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk

Heat the oven to 325 degrees.

Melt the butter in the bottom of an oblong Pyrex dish (9Ã—13 inch). Stir in the graham cracker crumbs and make a smooth bottom layer. Mix the remaining ingredients together and pour into pan. Bake on bottom shelf of oven until brown, about 25 minutes.

Cut in squares and keep in refrigerator.
