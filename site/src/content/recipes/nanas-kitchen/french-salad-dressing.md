title: French Salad Dressing
---

- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup canola oil
- 1/2 cup white vinegar
- 2/3 cup chili sauce
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- Juice of one lemon
- About 3 tablespoons grated onion
- 1 teaspoon paprika

Put all ingredients in a jar and shake really well.
