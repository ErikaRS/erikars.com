### Ingredients

- 2/3 c. butter, softened
- 3 1/2 c. flour (all-purpose)
- 4 tsp. baking powder
- 1 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. baking soda
- 1 1/2 c. buttermilk

### Instructions

Cut dry ingredients into butter. Add milk on floured surface. Knead lightly 20 times. Roll 1/2 inch thick. Cut with 2 inch biscuit cutter. Place with sides touching in greased pan. Bake 10 to 12 minutes at 450 degrees. Brown tops in broiler.
