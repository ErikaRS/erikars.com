### Ingredients

- 2 c flour
- Salt to taste
- 1 tsp sugar
- ½ c yogurt
- 2 tbsp oil
- ½ tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp baking soda
- 4 cloves garlic, grated

### Instructions

1. Mix ingredients, including half the garlic
2. With wet hands, knead into a soft dough
3. Once dough forms, oil hands and need into a smooth ball
4. Rest 10-15 minutes
5. Roll out a small ball
6. Sprinkle toppings on top (some of the reserved garlic, parsley, sesame seeds, etc)
7. Wet both sides, the bottom should be like glue
8. Put it in the hot iron skillet
9. When bubbles start to form, turn over and cook on the flame (it should stick). Or broil. Or maybe try a food torch.
10. Brush with butter and enjoy!
