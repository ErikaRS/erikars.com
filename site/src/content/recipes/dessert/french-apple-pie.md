**Category:** Desserts

### Ingredients

- 1 pie shell, unbaked
- 6 c sliced apples
- 3/4 c sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon

### Instructions

Mix sugar-cinnamon into apples. Spread apples into pie shell. Dot apples with butter.
