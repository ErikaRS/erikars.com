**Corn N Oil**

- 2 oz. aged rum
- 0.25–0.5 oz. Falernum, to taste
- 0.125–0.25 oz. lime juice
- 1 dash bitters

**Royal Bermuda Yacht Club**

- 1.5 oz. aged rum
- 0.25 oz. Cointreau
- 0.25 oz. falernum
- 0.25 oz. demerara syrup
- 0.75 oz. lime juice

**Mojito**

- 2 oz. [silver rum](https://robbreport.com/food-drink/spirits/21-best-rums-century-1234592807/)
- 0.75 oz. fresh lime juice
- 0.75 oz. simple syrup
- 10-12 mint leaves

**Dominicana**

- 1.5 oz. aged [rum](https://robbreport.com/tag/rum/)
- 1.5 oz. coffee liqueur
- ~2 oz. heavy cream, semi-whipped so its thicker but still pourable

**Queen’s Park Swizzle**

- 2 oz. aged rum
- 0.75 oz. lime juice
- 0.75 oz. demerara syrup
- 8-10 mint leaves

**Daiquiri**

- 2 oz. Plantation 3-Star White Rum
- 1 oz. fresh lime juice
- 0.75 oz. simple syrup

**Old Cuban**

- 1.5 oz. aged rum
- 0.75 oz. lime juice
- 0.75 oz. simple syrup
- 6-8 mint leaves
- 2 dashes Angostura Bitters
- 2 oz. sparkling wine

**Mai Tai**

- 2 oz. aged rum (Appleton Estates Signature Blend or Hamilton 86 Demerara Rum)
- 0.5 oz. orange curaçao (Grand Marnier)
- 0.5 oz. orgeat (Small Hands Foods Orgeat)
- 1 oz. lime juice

**Captain’s Blood**

- 2 oz. Jamaican rum
- 1 oz. lime juice
- 0.75 oz. simple syrup
- 2-3 solid dashes of Angostura bitters

**Airmail**

- 1 oz. rum
- 0.5 oz. lime juice
- 0.5 oz. honey syrup (2:1)
- 2 to 3 oz. sparkling wine

**Cable Car**

- 1.5 oz spiced or aged rum
- 0.5 oz orange Curaçao
- 0.75 oz lemon juice
- 0.5 oz (or less) simple syrup

**Brooklynite**

- 2 oz. [Jamaican rum](https://clicks.trx-hub.com/xid/pmc_0aaa4_robbreport?q=https%3A%2F%2Fgo.skimresources.com%3Fid%3D87443X1569519%26xs%3D1%26url%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fdrizly.com%252Fliquor%252Frum%252Faged-rum%252Ftwo-james-dr-bird-jamaica-rum%252Fp104522%26sref%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Frobbreport.com%252Ffood-drink%252Fspirits%252Flists%252Fbest-rum-cocktails-home-bartenders-1236874306%252F&p=https%3A%2F%2Frobbreport.com%2Ffood-drink%2Fspirits%2Flists%2Fbest-rum-cocktails-home-bartenders-1236874306%2F&ref=&event_type=click&trx-iid=iid1752516880679hdh)
- 0.75 oz. lime juice
- 0.75 oz. honey syrup
- 1 dash [bitters](https://www.astorwines.com/item/22600)
