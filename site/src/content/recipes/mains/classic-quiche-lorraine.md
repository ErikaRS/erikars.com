From *Cooking for Dummies* by Bryan Miller, Marie Rama

Place oven rack in lowest position. Preheat to 375F.

Heat skillet over medium heat. Sautee until crisp  
**➜** **3 strips bacon**  
Remove and drain on paper towels.

In the same pan, reserve  
**➜** **1 tbsp bacon fat**  
and cook until wilted  
****➜**** **1 small onion, diced**

In   
**➜** **one 9-inch pie crust in pan**  
crumble bacon, add onion and  
**➜ 1/2 c cubed Gruyere or Swiss cheese**

In a bowl, whisk together  
**➜ 3 eggs, lightly beaten**  
**➜** **1/2 c heavy cream or half-and-half**  
**➜** **1/2 c milk**  
**➜** **2 tbsp minced parsley**  
**➜** **1/4 tsp nutmeg**  
**➜** **1/4 tsp salt**  
**➜** **1/4 tsp white pepper**

Pour egg mixture into pie crust.

Place on baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes, until firm.
