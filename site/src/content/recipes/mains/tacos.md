Dice   
**➜ 1 onion**
Either microwave caramelize the onion; or saute in a pan with oil or butter.

Brown
**➜ 1 lb of ground beef**
Once browned, add the onion.
Add
**➜ 2 cloves garlic, diced**
**➜ 2 Tbsp cumin powder**
**➜ 1 Tbsp chili powder**
**➜ 1 tsp paprika**
**➜ 2 tsp oregano**
**➜ Black pepper and salt to taste**
Saute for a minute

Add
**➜ 1/2c water**
Mix well, cook briefly.
Add
**➜ 1 10oz bag frozen corn**
Cook until warmed through.
If desired, cook longer to crisp beef and enhance flavors
