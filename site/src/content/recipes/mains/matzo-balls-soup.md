**Ingredients**

- 4 tbsp fat (schmaltz or vegetable oil)
- 4 eggs lightly beaten
- 1 cup matzo meal
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 cup soup stock or water

**Recipe**

Mix fat and eggs together. Add matzo meal and salt. When blended add soup stock or water. Cover mixture in refrigerator for at least 20-30 minutes. Using a 2-3 quart pot, bring salt[ed?] water to boil and drop in balls of matzo mixture. Cover pot and cook 30-40 minutes. Makes 8 balls.

Note: it is easier to shape the balls if you use wet hands.

(Delicious as a substitute for noodles in chicken noodle soup)

**Soup ingredients**

- 1 whole chicken
- 1 large onion peeled
- 2 or 3 carrots peeled and cut into 1 inch pieces
- 2 parsnips peeled and cut into 1 inch pieces
- 2 celery stalks cut in half
- 1 bunch of fresh dill
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp white pepper
