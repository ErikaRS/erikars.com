title: Stuffed Pepper Rice
---

Cook  
**1 lb of sausage**  
Remove from pan and drain on paper towel

Saute in oil  
**1 onion**  
**1 head of fennel (optional)**  
**Garlic (to taste)**

Add & saute  
**1.5 lbs sweet/bell peppers**

Re-add cooked sausage

Add & saute  
**Dried basil**  
**Dried oregano**  
**Paprika**  
**Salt & pepper**  
**1 can tomato paste**

Add & break up  
**2 cups (measured dry) rice, cooked**

Add & mix  
**28oz can crushed tomatoes**

Top with  
**Grated cheddar cheese**

Either cover and cook slowly to melt cheese and form bottom crust; or put pan in oven (~350F) to melt & brown cheese
