From *Healthy Homestyle Cooking* by Evelyn Tribole

Bring water to boil in a large saucepan.

Meanwhile, cut lengthwise in half  
**➜ 2 large green peppers**  
Remove and discard the stems, seeds, and membranes.

Place the peppers in the boiling water for 3 minutes. Remove with slotted spoon, setting them inverted on paper towels to drain.

Preheat the oven to 375F.

Saute until turkey is cooked  
****➜**** **8 oz ground turkey**  
**➜** **1/2 c chopped onions**

Stir in  
**➜** **1 c cooked brown rice**  
**➜** **1 c stewed tomatoes** **(with juices)**  
**➜** **2 tbsp tomato paste**  
****➜**** **1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce**  
****➜**** **1/2 tsp dried basil**  
****➜**** **1/4 tsp salt**  
**➜** **1/4 tsp ground black pepper**  
Cover and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Place the pepper halves in a baking dish. Spoon in meat mixture. Sprinkle with  
**➜** **1/4 c finely shredded cheddar cheese**

Bake about 10 minutes, until heated through and cheese melted.
