title: Taco Bake

**Category:** Main Dishes

### Ingredients

- 1 lb beef, ground
- 12 oz. egg noodles (medium)
- 1 envelope taco seasoning mix
- 1 c. water
- 1 c. corn, frozen (1/2 – 1cup)
- 1 small can black olives, sliced
- 8 oz. cheddar cheese, shredded

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 350F.

Cook noodles.

Brown ground beef. Drain fat. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Add corn. Bring to boil. Simmer until nearly dry. Mix in olives.

Mix beef/corn mixture with noodles. Spread in 13″ x 9″ baking dish. Top with cheese (optional: broken tortilla chips under cheese). Bake for 30 minutes.
