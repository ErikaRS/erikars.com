From *Cooking for Dummies* by Bryan Miller, Marie Rama

Butter the bottom and sides of a 2 1/2 – 3 quart shallow baking dish.

Slice   
**➜ 8 oz loaf of seedless Italian bread**  
If needed, dry slices in the oven. Arrange the slices in the baking dish, overlapping edges slightly.

Saute until crisp  
**➜** **5 slices bacon**  
Drain on paper towels and crumble when cool.

Sprinkle bacon over the bread slices. Top with  
**➜** **1 1/2 c grated Gouda, Gruyere, or fontina**  
**➜** **1/2 c spinache or sorrel leaves**

In a bowl, mix  
**➜** **5 eggs, lightly beaten**  
**➜** **2 c milk**  
**➜** **2 tbsp tomato-based salsa**  
**➜** **salt and pepper to taste**

Pour egg mixture over the bread and let sit 15 minutes.

Preheat oven to 350F. Bake strata for 30 minutes or until set.
