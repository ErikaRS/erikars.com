### INGREDIENTS
- 3 T extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 T white wine vinegar
- 1 fennel bulb, stalks discarded, halved, cored and cut into 1/4-inch dice
- 2 apples, cut into 1/4-inch dice
- 1/2 small red onion, chopped fine
- 1 head lettuce cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1/2 cup walnuts, toasted and chopped
- 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled (1 cup)
- salt and pepper

### PREPARATION

Whisk oil and vinegar together in a large bowl. Add fennel, apples and onion; toss and let stand at room temperature to blend flavors, 5 minutes.
Add lettuce and walnuts; toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Top with goat cheese and serve.
